EZ-Roll


Key Points
  • Second Place!
  • Designed, sketched, and 3D printed a wheelchair lever using SOLIDWORKS
  • Device utilizes biceps as means of movement for users whose triceps, posterior deltoids, and flexor muscles are inhibited
  • Built a reaching tool for patients in wheelchairs to enable them to pick up items (i.e keys) from the ground. It can also be used as an accelerator to steer your wheelchair without getting your hands dirty
  • Contributors: Enrique Moran, Stephanie Chen, Chaehyeon Lee, Claire Song, Dannon Sturn, Navneeth Devadas, and Joshua Park
